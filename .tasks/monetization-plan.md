## 💵 PHASE 2: PREMIUM TIER (Month 3-4)

### **DevCard Pro — $3 one-time payment**

**Why One-Time vs Subscription?**
- Lower barrier to entry
- Feels like buying a "power-up" (gaming psychology)
- No churn risk
- Viral sharing continues (no paywall on viewing)

### **Pro Features:**

#### **1. Remove Watermark**
- Free: "Made with DevCard 3D" badge
- Pro: Clean card, full branding control

#### **2. Exclusive Themes (5 more)**
- Diamond Prism (rainbow holographic)
- Nebula Dark (cosmic purple/blue)
- Emerald Matrix (Matrix-style green)
- Ruby Fire (intense red/orange)
- Arctic Frost (icy blue/white)

#### **3. HD Export**
- Free: 330x500px
- Pro: 2x resolution (660x1000px) for print quality

#### **4. Priority Support**
- Direct email support
- Feature request priority

#### **5. Early Access**
- Beta features before public release

### **Pricing Psychology:**
- Not $2.99 (feels cheap)
- Not $5 (feels too much for one-time)
- **$3** = Coffee price, impulse buy
